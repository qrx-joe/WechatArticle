AI 是一面镜子 — 放大你的潜意识偏好

Professional hand-drawn sketchnote framework on pure white. Black ink line work with slight wobble, à la Mike Rohde sketchnoting.

PALETTE: mono-ink — black ink dominant, sparse semantic accents
COLORS: Pure White background (#FFFFFF), Near Black (#1A1A1A) for all outlines, text, figures, arrows; Coral Red (#E8655A) for the perfectionism/hidden desire side; Muted Teal (#5FA8A8) for the pragmatic/true intent side. Color accents under 10% of canvas.

STRUCTURE: Central mirror/framework concept.

CENTER: A large hand-drawn mirror frame (oval or rounded-rectangle). The mirror is labeled "AI 编程助手" in bold hand-lettered text across the top frame.

LEFT OF MIRROR: A stick figure person walking toward the mirror with two thought bubbles:
- Thought 1 (teal accent): "先做出来看看" — labeled "第一次的真实想法"
- Thought 2 (red accent): "这次一定要做好" — labeled "第二次的真实想法"

RIGHT OF MIRROR (the reflection): The mirror reflects not a face but abstract concepts amplified:
- Reflection 1 (teal): Pragmatic icons — running gears, quick prototype, "Done > Perfect"
- Reflection 2 (red): Perfectionist icons — rule books, crossed-out drafts, "Perfect > Done"

BOTTOM BRIDGE: A curved hand-drawn arrow connecting left to right, labeled "AI 放大了你已经有的偏好" in handwritten text.

BOTTOM: Single-line hand-lettered tagline: "不是 AI 选择了风格，是 AI 放大了你的偏好"

STYLE: Hand-lettered headings (bold, oversized), handwritten body annotations, generous white space, no computer fonts, no gradients, no shadows. Human figures: simplified stylized silhouettes, not photorealistic.

Clean composition with generous white space. Simple pure white background.

Include a subtle watermark "LumiQ Lab" positioned at bottom-right.

ASPECT: 16:9, moderate complexity
