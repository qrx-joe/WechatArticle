四个教训 — 迭代比重写更勇敢

Professional hand-drawn sketchnote infographic on pure white. Black ink line work with slight wobble, à la Mike Rohde sketchnoting.

PALETTE: mono-ink — black ink dominant, sparse semantic accents
COLORS: Pure White background (#FFFFFF), Near Black (#1A1A1A) for all outlines, text, figures, arrows; Coral Red (#E8655A) for problem/risk highlights; Muted Teal (#5FA8A8) for solution/positive highlights. Color accents under 10% of canvas.

LAYOUT: 2x2 grid of rounded-rectangle cards with hand-lettered content.

TOP-LEFT CARD: Title "重来 ≠ 更好" (hand-lettered, bold). Small icon: a circular arrow chasing its own tail. Handwritten annotation: "重做只让你看到新版的问题". A tiny stick figure looking at v1 problems, then at v2 problems — same expression.

TOP-RIGHT CARD: Title "规范 ≠ 工程" (hand-lettered, bold). Small icon: a beautiful document next to a broken gear. Handwritten annotation: "文档完美 ≠ 代码能活". Teal checkmark next to "工程实践", red X next to "只写规范".

BOTTOM-LEFT CARD: Title "风险 = 完美主义" (hand-lettered, bold). Small icon: a stick figure opening a second repo while the first one works fine. Handwritten annotation: "AI 没逼你，是你自己的完美主义". Red accent underline on "完美主义".

BOTTOM-RIGHT CARD: Title "够好 > 完美" (hand-lettered, bold, teal accent). Small icon: a working engine (slightly messy) next to a polished but empty frame. Handwritten annotation: "优雅是奢侈品，可用是必需品". Teal highlight on "可用".

BOTTOM: Large hand-lettered tagline spanning full width: "迭代比重写更勇敢。承认够好比追求完美更需要智慧。"

STYLE: Hand-lettered headings (bold, oversized), handwritten body annotations, generous white space, no computer fonts, no gradients, no shadows.

Clean composition with generous white space. Simple pure white background. Main elements centered.

Include a subtle watermark "LumiQ Lab" positioned at bottom-right.
